About Vetina

Vetina is leading Animal Health Care product and Value-added service providers to the Veterinary professionals in India. Our presence is Pan India.

We are partnering with leading Logistic providers in the Country ‘’ Parekh Integrated services Pvt Ltd ‘’ having in business for more than 40 years and have more than 350 own Distribution centers across India.

We are dedicated to advancing the world of Veterinary medicine, Nutritional products, and all the wellbeing needs of Veterinary professions , empowering healthcare teams across the companion , Ruminants , poultry, aqua health markets.

We provide a comprehensive suite of products, software, and services to help drive improved patient health, strong client relationships, and successful financial outcomes for veterinary professionals.

Veterinarians are at the heart of all we do. We are here for you and because of you

Our Vision

To be recognized as preferred partners in India by Veterinary professionals and Animal owners

Our Mission

To meet all the Animal health and well being related needs of both the Veterinary professionals and Animal owners.
