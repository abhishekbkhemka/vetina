Vetina VETbuddy

Veterinary Practice Management Software

VETINA VETbuddy is a Veterinary Practice Management Software driven by it's powerful and flexible EMR system. It is a web and cloud based system which helps you to keep all the data of the patients & the clinic, at one place. This is easily accessible from any place or device. VETINA VETbuddy takes care of all your billing, inventory, reports, etc. and helps to make your work easier.
